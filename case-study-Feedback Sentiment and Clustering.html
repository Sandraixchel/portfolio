Featured case study

Feedback Sentiment and Clustering

Problem: OLIST needed to understand why customers were satisfied or dissatisfied with their platform. Solution: Analyzed 42,000+ customer reviews using Python and Power BI to identify sentiment drivers and root causes. Discovered that delivery performance is the #1 satisfaction factor and delivery delays drive 60%+ of negative feedback. Provided data-driven recommendations that could improve customer retention and operational efficiency.

  • Power BI
  • Python
  • VADER
  • Azure Translator Service
Timeline: 4 days · November 2025 Dataset: 42k e-commerce reviews · 2GB raw size

Explore the live dashboard

Filter by region, time period, and keyword themes to uncover customer satisfaction patterns. Best viewed in full-screen.

Approach

Loaded 42,000+ customer reviews from OLIST Brazilian e-Commerce dataset and translated Portuguese comments to English using Azure Cognitive Services. Performed sentiment classification using VADER to categorize reviews as positive (>0.5), neutral (0-0.5), and negative (<0). Extracted meaningful words by removing stopwords and filtering for 2+ character words, then analyzed word frequencies to identify top 100 keywords per sentiment category. Visualized clustering results in Power BI using word clouds and trend charts to reveal key satisfaction drivers and dissatisfaction factors.

Highlights

Identified delivery performance as the #1 driver of customer satisfaction. Delivery delays accounted for 61% of negative feedback across all regions

  • Delivery complaints represented 61% of all negative sentiment regardless of region.
  • Geographic analysis exposed São Paulo as top performer while Rio de Janeiro and Belo Horizonte showed opportunity for operational improvement
  • Seasonal trends revealed spikes in negative feedback during high-demand periods like Black Friday, indicating need for enhanced logistics planning

What I would improve next

Upgrade from VADER to BERT-based sentiment analysis for improved accuracy on nuanced customer emotions and mixed sentiment reviews . Implement automated real-time alerts to flag emerging negative sentiment trends before they escalate. Additionally, extend clustering analysis to specific product categories to identify category-specific satisfaction drivers and tailor recommendations by product type.
