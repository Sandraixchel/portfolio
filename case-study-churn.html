Featured case study

Subscription Churn Prediction

I engineered behavioral features, trained tree-based models, and translated the findings into a BI story that equips customer-success managers with proactive win-back playbooks.

  • Python
  • Pandas
  • scikit-learn
  • Power BI
Timeline: 2 weeks · Aug 2025 Dataset: 58k subscribers · 36 engineered features

Watch the model insights

The embedded BI view tracks chorded SHAP explanations, risk cohorts, and cohort-level KPIs. Toggle the slicers to contrast model drivers by subscription age.

Model build

After cleaning subscriber tenure, add-on usage, and ticket history, I trained LightGBM and CatBoost models on a stratified 75/25 split. The final stacked ensemble hit ROC-AUC 0.87 and surfaced easy-to-act drivers such as first-response latency.

Business impact

  • Segment-based playbooks reduced involuntary churn by 6% in the pilot region within one quarter.
  • Weekly refresh pipeline (dbt + Power BI) cut analyst time from 4 hours to 20 minutes.
  • CSM leadership adopted the Explainability page as the default win-back standup artifact.

Next steps
