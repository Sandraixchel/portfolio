Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of movie box office performance, examining worldwide revenues, domestic performance, and international appeal across multiple film genres and major studios. The dataset encompasses over 50 theatrical releases from 1977 to 2022, offering insights into what drives commercial success in Hollywood.

Highlights

  • Disney dominates the dataset, with films consistently generating $1B+ in worldwide revenue
  • Action and Sci-Fi genres command the highest worldwide revenues, with Star Wars and Marvel franchises leading
  • Animation shows consistent performance with lower production risk and strong profitability
  • International markets typically represent 60-70% of total revenue for major releases
  • Disney's stranglehold on the market is evident, particularly post-2009
